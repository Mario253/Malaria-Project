More Info

Malaria is a blood borne disease caused by mosquitoes. This is a very deadly disease with nearly half the world at risk. Africa has the most diagnosis and deaths to this disease. The US only gets 1,700 cases a year. In 2017, 219 million were diagnosed at 435,000 died. This is one of the most deadly and popular diseases in the word. This disease is deadly but if treated in 2 weeks, the disease can be cured. Mostly children are at risk of this disease, especially infants.
